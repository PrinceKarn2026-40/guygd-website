Who We Are

Gbeh-lay United Youths for Growth and Development (GUYGD) is a youth-led organization founded in Gbeh-lay, Liberia. We are committed to uniting young people and channeling their energy toward education, leadership, and sustainable community development.

Since our founding, we have grown into a vibrant community of passionate young people who believe in the power of collective action to transform lives and communities.

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To unite the youths of Gbeh-lay and empower them through education, skills development, and community service to become agents of positive change.

🔭

Our Vision

A Gbeh-lay where every young person has the opportunity, resources, and support to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

💎

Our History

2018

Founded

GUYGD was established by a group of passionate youths in Gbeh-lay with a vision to unite and empower their peers.

2021

Community Expansion

Expanded our reach to neighboring communities and grew membership to over 100 active members.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our official website to better serve members and connect with supporters worldwide.
