Our Programs

Initiatives designed to empower, educate, and uplift our community.

🛠️

Skills Development

We run vocational and skills training workshops to equip youths with practical abilities that translate into employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.

  • Computer literacy and digital skills
  • Entrepreneurship and business training
  • Agricultural and trade skills workshops
🌍

Community Service

Our members regularly engage in community service activities that improve the quality of life in Gbeh-lay and surrounding areas.

  • Community clean-up campaigns
  • Tree planting and environmental awareness
  • Health and sanitation drives
🏆

Leadership Development

We invest in developing the next generation of leaders through structured training, mentorship, and opportunities to lead real projects.

  • Youth leadership summits
  • Public speaking and debate training
  • Mentorship with community elders and professionals
⚽

Sports & Recreation

We organize sports tournaments and recreational activities to promote teamwork, discipline, and healthy living among youths.

  • Annual football tournament
  • Indoor games and cultural activities
  • Youth talent showcases
